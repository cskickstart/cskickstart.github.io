Hey KickStart alumni! Want to gain experience and a behind-the-scenes look into the planning of CS KickStart? Join the team of CS KickStart organizers! Throughout the planning process, you'll have the chance to connect with other CS KickStart alumni, our sponsor companies, and leaders on campus. You'll work with the EECS department and many other people who are passionate about empowering women in STEM fields. If you're interested in helping out, please contact the organizers at cskickstart@gmail.com. We'd love to have you on the team!
