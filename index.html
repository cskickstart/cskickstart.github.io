Curious about computer science?

CS KickStart is a week-long program introducing students to computer science while meeting other computer science students and professionals.

Students

This program primarily targets women who are interested in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and math.

Experience

Partcipants will get hands-on experience in programming introducing them to the creativity and diversity of computer science.

Exposure

Particpants will also get the opportunity to visit a tech company in the Bay Area to see what life is like for computer scientists in industry.
